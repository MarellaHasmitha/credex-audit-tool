# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Product

Credex — SaaS Audit Tool

---

## Problem

Many students, freelancers, startups, and small teams spend too much on SaaS tools without comparing better alternatives.

Credex helps users:

- Track software spending
- Detect overspending
- Discover cheaper alternatives
- Estimate monthly and annual savings

---

## Target Users

Primary users:

- Freelancers
- Students building startups
- Small businesses
- Startup founders

---

## Launch Strategy

To get early users, I would start with:

- LinkedIn posts
- Developer communities
- Startup groups
- Founder networks

The goal is to collect real feedback from early users.

---

## Revenue Model

Possible monetization:

### Free Plan

Basic SaaS audit for individuals.

### Pro Plan

Advanced analytics, team reports, and unlimited audits.

---

## Success Metrics

I would measure:

- Number of users
- Number of audits created
- Average savings discovered
- Returning users

---

## Future Vision

Future improvements:

- Real-time pricing APIs
- AI-powered recommendations
- Team collaboration
