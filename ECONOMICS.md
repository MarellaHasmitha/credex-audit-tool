# Economics

## Product

Credex — SaaS Audit Tool

---

## Problem

Many individuals, startups, and small teams overspend on SaaS tools because they do not regularly review subscriptions or compare alternatives.

This leads to unnecessary monthly software expenses.

---

## Economic Value

Credex helps users:

- Identify unused or expensive subscriptions
- Discover cheaper alternatives
- Reduce monthly SaaS costs
- Improve software budget planning

Example:

If a team saves $20–$100 per month, the product creates immediate financial value.

---

## Why Users Would Pay

Users may pay for Credex because it helps them save more money than the product costs.

Example:

If Credex helps save $100/month, paying $10–$20/month can still be valuable.

---

## Possible Revenue Model

### Free Plan

Basic SaaS audit for individual users.

### Pro Plan

Advanced analytics, team reports, and unlimited audits.

---

## Future Opportunity

As more companies use multiple SaaS tools, cost optimization platforms like Credex can become increasingly valuable.
