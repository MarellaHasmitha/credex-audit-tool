# User Interview

## User 1 — Startup Founder

### Problem

Managing multiple SaaS subscriptions is expensive.

Sometimes team plans are purchased without comparing cheaper alternatives.

### Need

A simple way to track software spending and identify savings.

### Product Decision

Because of this, I added:

- Cost comparison
- Alternative plan suggestions

---

## User 2 — Freelancer

### Problem

Uses many productivity and design tools but does not track total monthly spending.

### Need

A quick way to understand where money is being spent.

### Product Decision

Because of this, I added:

- Monthly savings calculation
- Annual savings calculation

---

## Conclusion

These user insights helped shape Credex as a SaaS cost optimization tool for individuals and small teams.
