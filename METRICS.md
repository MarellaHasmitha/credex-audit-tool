# Product Metrics

## Objective

Measure whether Credex helps users reduce SaaS spending and improve software decisions.

---

## Key Metrics

### Users

Number of users who use the audit tool.

Goal:

Track product adoption.

---

### Audits Created

Number of audit reports generated.

Goal:

Measure product usage.

---

### Savings Identified

Total cost savings discovered through audits.

Goal:

Measure product value.

---

### Shared Reports

Number of shareable audit links created.

Goal:

Measure collaboration and engagement.

---

### Returning Users

Users who come back and perform audits again.

Goal:

Measure product usefulness and retention.

---

## Success Definition

If users regularly create audits, discover savings, and return to the platform, Credex is solving a real problem.
